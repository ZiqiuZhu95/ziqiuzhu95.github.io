About Me

I'm glad you're here!

My name is Ziq (Pronounced as Zeke), and I just graduated from University of Waterloo and Wilfrid Laurier University under the Mathematics and Business Administration Double Degree program. My main inspiration is to be in research as a statistician/economist, but as an aside, I am a hobbyist photographer with a passion for travelling and capturing the amazing landscapes I visit and the stunning people I meet.

I bought my first DSLR in January 2017 in anticipation of my study abroad term at the University of Hong Kong. Ever since, I've spent a lot of time learning capture and post-processing techniques, which you can see in my portfolio. Over time, I found myself enchanted with the creative flexibility of photography, especially when it came down to photo manipulation with Adobe Lightroom and Photoshop. While I consider myself to be only at an intermediate-beginner level, I plan on developing my techniques as I continue to travel and take pictures in the future. You'll find that most of the pictures I decide to post on instagram are heavily edited.

Gear I use: Canon Rebel T5i, Canon EF-S 50mm, Canon EF-S 24MM 1.2.8 STM, and Canon EF-S 10-18mm f/4.5-5.6 IS STM Lens

Within the next few years, I plan on purchasing a new camera once I find a stable job, so I'll make do with what I have until then!
